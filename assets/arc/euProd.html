8 + 2 Hour SAFE Comprehensive Live 2018 Course Overview

This year The Knowledge Coop takes a crack at envisioning a new mortgage industry by analyzing the status quo, identifying some of the issues and struggles companies have had with growing too fast that led to damaged cultures and consumer harm, and then creating a plan for a new industry that reaches out to all groups to provide credit to everybody who qualifies. As always, the class has been developed by pouring through the most recent data available and diving deep into a solutions based class with plenty of interaction and our famous gameshow!
